Donkey Kong Run Game

Swing Through the Jungle Adventure

Jump. Swing. Collect. Adventure.

Join Donkey Kong on a thrilling jungle adventure! Swing from vine to vine, dodge dangerous obstacles, and collect bananas while avoiding the dangers of the jungle.

FEATURES

Exciting Jungle Levels

Swing through lush jungle landscapes with stunning visuals and challenging platforms.

Unlock Special Abilities

Gain new powers and abilities as you progress through the jungle and face more challenges.

Upbeat Jungle Music

Enjoy energetic and fun jungle tunes that keep the adventure lively and exciting.

Epic Boss Fights

Face off against giant jungle bosses in thrilling battles that will test your skills.

HOW TO PLAY

1

Start Your Jungle Adventure

Begin your journey in the wild jungle, filled with vines, obstacles, and hidden treasures.

2

Swing and Jump

Use arrow keys to move and spacebar to swing from vine to vine, avoiding dangers in your path.

3

Collect Bananas

Gather bananas along your journey to unlock new abilities and increase your score.

4

Avoid Obstacles

Dodge treacherous obstacles like falling rocks, traps, and wild animals as you swing through the jungle.

5

Complete the Adventure

Finish all levels to complete the adventure and become the ultimate jungle explorer!
